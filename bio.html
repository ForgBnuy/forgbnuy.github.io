Andrzej Śleszyński is a designer, composer, and producer based in Poland. His work is defined by high-contrast minimalism and the creation of intentional visual and sonic environments.

Operating under the alias witbezkacy, he explores electronic landscapes via Logic Pro. His 2024 discography includes the album Skąpiec and the single AYA.
